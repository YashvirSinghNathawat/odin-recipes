Poha

Poha is a breakfast recipe which is very simple to make and taste delicious

Indegrients :

  • Poha 1 bowl
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Tomotoes
  • Chopped Chillies
  • Oil
  • Rai
  • Chopped Coriander Leaves

Procedure:

  1. Rinse poha in a bowl.
  2. In a pot, add oil.
  3. Add Rai,and some curry leaves
  4. Add veggies in the pot
  5. After 1-2 min,add poha
  6. Add masalas and salt according to taste
  7. Garnish poha with coriander leaves

Masala Poha is ready!
