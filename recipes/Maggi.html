Masala Maggi

Masala Maggi is a instant recipe which is very simple to make and taste delicious.It can be use for all day and favourite for bachelors

Indegrients :

  • Masala Maggi
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Tomotoes
  • Chopped Chillies
  • Oil
  • Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • Water

Procedure:

  1. In a pot, add oil.
  2. Add veggies in the pot
  3. After 1-2 min,add 1 cup water
  4. Add maggi in the pot
  5. Add masalas and salt according to taste
  6. Bowl for 2 min
  7. Garnish poha with coriander leaves

Masala Maggi is ready!
