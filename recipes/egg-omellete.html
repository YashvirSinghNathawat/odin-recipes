Egg-Omellete

Egg Omellete is a non-vegetarian dish which is quite famous.In this tutorial we will make Indian version which is both tasty and spicy.

Indegrients:-

  1. Eggs 2
  2. Chopped Onions
  3. Chopped Chili
  4. Ginger Garlic Paste
  5. Oil
  6. Chopped Coriander
  7. Garam Masala
  8. Salt

Procedure :-

  1. Take 2 eggs in a bowl
  2. Add some onions,chili and coriander
  3. Pour garam masala and salt to taste
  4. After paste is ready,lit up the gas and put on the pan on medium flame
  5. Add some oil and wait for 1 min
  6. After pour the paste on pan gently(pour it on the center for round shapes)
  7. Add some oil on edges and with the help of handle try to flip the omellete carefully without burning the omellete
  8. Carefully take out the omellete when it is cooked from both sides

Omelette is ready to serve!
